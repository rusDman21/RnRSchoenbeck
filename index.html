Our Story

Our story begins in 2010 in a little hair salon in the Lincoln Park neighborhood of Chicago where Renee was working with Sara, Russell’s stepmom. Sara often talked to Renee about Russell, her hilarious and smart stepson who lived in Michigan. Finally, Renee’s interest was peaked, and she decided to send Russell a Facebook message. Over the next few months, Renee and Russell traveled between Michigan and Illinois, and eventually, Russell moved back to his hometown of Chicago. Russell and Renee moved in to a charming little Wicker Park apartment in May of 2012. On Friday, August 30, 2013, Russell and Renee got engaged! After much discussion and exploration, we decided to make Colorado our new home and moved to Broomfield in April of 2014. We are so excited for our future together and can’t wait to share our special day with you!

Attire

What to Wear:

March is Colorado’s snowiest month, and Allie’s Cabin is only accessible by sleigh. Be sure to wear your coat, bring snow boots, and don’t forget your mittens! There is a boot room in the cabin where you can store your warm clothes and change into your dancing shoes!

Thursday and Saturday are Casual. Dress comfortably.

Friday (our wedding day) is Creative Black Tie Optional. Basically, have some fun with your formal wear. See below for suggestions:
Women: Floor-length gown, knee-length cocktail dress, your dressiest little black dress, a dressy suit or dressy separates. Have fun with accessories – don’t be afraid of bright colors!
Men: Definitely rock a dark suit or maybe even a tux. Get creative with a colored or patterned dress shirt, tie, or bow tie. Maybe add some interesting accessories: cuff links, tie clip, pocket square, or watch!

Transportation

Flying in?

Guests from out of town should plan to fly in to either Denver International Airport (DEN) or Eagle County Airport (EGE).

Don’t want to drive?

We recommend Colorado Mountain Express for your trip to and from the airport. Mention that you are in town for a wedding and receive 20% off of your fare! The trip from DIA to the lodge is about 2 hours, and from EGE is about 30 minutes. Once you arrive at The Christie Lodge, there is a complimentary shuttle to and from Beaver Creek Village, Vail Mountain and around downtown Avon. There are also complimentary shuttles within each of those areas. On the day of the wedding, we will provide transportation for you to and from the wedding events.

Up for a drive?

If you choose to drive, parking is free at the Christie Lodge. It is about a 2 hour drive from Denver International Airport, and about a 30 minute drive from Eagle County Airport.

From Denver International Airport:

Follow the main road out of the airport. Join I-70 west towards “Grand Junction”. Follow I-70 west for approximately 110-120 miles to exit 167, “AVON”. Get off at exit 167 – Make a left turn (third exit) at the third main roundabout south of I-70, the one with the 4 large galloping horse sculptures in the center, and the Christie Lodge is on your left.

From Eagle County Airport:

Turn right out of the airport area and travel east on US-6. Turn left onto Eby Creek Road. Take I-70 E towards Denver. Exit 167/Avon. Turn right at the bottom of the exit ramp. The Christie Lodge will be on the left at 47 East Beaver Creek Boulevard. Exit three-fourths of the way around the roundabout to reach the entrance.
